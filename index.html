About Us

Around the world, Kwality Wall's creates moments of happiness every day for people in communities everywhere and is celebrating this through the official sponsorship of the UN Sustainable Development Solutions Network (SDSN) World Happiness Report–which can be read here. Kwality Wall's has brought people together over ice cream, spreading happiness through social connection. The sponsorship is the latest step in the brand’s commitment to raising national happiness levels and is part of Kwality Wall's ambition to highlight the importance of social connections between people as a driver of happiness levels.
